The Game of Life

Cells floating in the great sea of life... in plain Javascript.

Conway's Rules:

A living cell:

  • With fewer than two living neighbors dies (isolation).
  • With two or three living neighbors survives (community).
  • With more than three living neighbors dies (overpopulation).

An empty spot:

  • Will be hospitable to new life if it has three living neighbors (reproduction).
